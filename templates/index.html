About Hogwarts

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a renowned magical school located in the Highlands of Scotland. It is a place where young witches and wizards from all over the world come to learn and hone their magical abilities. With its rich history and esteemed faculty, Hogwarts offers a unique and immersive educational experience.

At Hogwarts, students are sorted into one of four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin. Each house has its own distinct traits and values, fostering a sense of camaraderie and competition among the students.
